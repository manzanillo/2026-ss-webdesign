The Band

Alice "The Bottleneck" Smith

Lead Vocals

Alice has been delivering render-blocking harmonies since dial-up was a thing. Her voice can stall a browser mid-paint and still earn a standing ovation.

Bob "Latency" Jones

Lead Guitar

Bob's riffs arrive exactly 300 ms after you expect them — and somehow that makes them hit harder. He calls it "the art of deferred resolution".

Carol "Blocking" Chen

Drums & Percussion

Carol's syncopated blocking patterns have crashed more than one browser tab. She calls it "percussive garbage collection". The crowd calls it transcendent.
